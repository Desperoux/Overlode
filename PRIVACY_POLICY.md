Overlode Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overlode does not collect, store, or share personal information.

All workout data, bodyweight records, routines, and settings are stored locally on your device only.

Overlode currently:

does not require an account
does not use cloud storage
does not sell user data
does not share data with third parties
does not contain advertising

Optional backup exports are created manually by the user and remain under the user's control.

If analytics, accounts, or cloud features are added in future versions, this privacy policy may be updated.
